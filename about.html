Our Story

Probity Partners East Africa

Formerly known as Bona Fide Law Chambers, we are a regional law firm serving East Africa, headquartered in Kigali, Rwanda since 2011.

Our firm is composed of a multi-lingual, multi-disciplinary group of professionals with vast experience in Access to Justice, Rule of Law, Human Rights, Good Governance, and Legal Aid.
