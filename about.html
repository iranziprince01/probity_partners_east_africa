Our Identity

A regional law firm serving East Africa with excellence and integrity

Probity Partners East Africa (PPEA), formerly known as Bona Fide Law Chambers, is a regional law firm serving East Africa. The firm, headquartered in Kigali, Rwanda, was established in 2011. PPEA is composed of a multi-lingual, multi-disciplinary group of professionals with vast experience in the fields of Access to Justice, Rule of Law, Human Rights, Good Governance and Legal Aid from the region and around the world.

PPEA boasts a team of highly educated and highly efficient attorneys and consultants and guarantees the timely completion of the highest standard of work. Our approach is human rights based and we are solution-oriented and pragmatic. We also boast expertise in civil and criminal litigation, legal advice and assistance and research through data collection and analysis. We also advice CSOs and development partners on a number of legal issues pertaining to legal and policy frameworks in Rwanda.
