Professional Experience

BAKUNDA Emmanuel is an ambitious Corporate Associate Lawyer with a diverse educational background. He obtained his Bachelor's Degree in Law from the esteemed University of Rwanda, where he laid the foundation for his legal career. Following his undergraduate studies, Emmanuel pursued a Diploma in Legal Practice at the Institute of Legal Practice and Development, acquiring practical skills and knowledge in legal matters.

Currently, Emmanuel is furthering his academic pursuit by undertaking a Master's degree in Business Law at the University of Rwanda to deepen his expertise in corporate legal matters. With a passion for corporate law and a dedication to his professional growth, Emmanuel is a legal drafter, an Intellectual property Rights expert and a proficient legal advisor in the corporate sector, contributing towards the success and compliance of businesses.
