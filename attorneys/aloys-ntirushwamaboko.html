Biography

Aloys NTIRUSHWAMABOKO is a distinguished senior advocate with over 17 years of experience in the legal field. Known for his expertise in family law and criminal defense, he has built a reputation for providing exceptional legal representation and guidance to clients in complex litigation. He has a law bachelor's degree and a legal practice diploma. He is also member to the East Africa Law Society and the Rwanda Bar Association.

Over the years, Aloys has been involved in a number of cases, research projects, and advocacy efforts that have influenced the justice sector. He started his work in non-governmental organizations. Throughout his career, Aloys has successfully handled high-profile criminal cases and complex divorce proceedings.

Aloys believes in a client-centered approach, ensuring that each individual receives personalized attention and a comprehensive legal strategy tailored to their needs.
